Our Story

A Small Town, A Big Harvest.

ME Cashews grows out of the cashew belt near Jayamkondam, where Gangaikonda Cholapuram (Brihadisvara Temple) — located just 8 km from the town center, this 11th-century architectural marvel built by Rajendra Chola — has been a way of life for generations.

We work directly with growers and processors in our own town, sorting and grading every harvest ourselves rather than buying mixed lots — which is why our sizing stays consistent order after order.

What started as a small family operation now ships across India, but the standard hasn't moved: open the bag, and the cashew should look exactly like what you were promised.
